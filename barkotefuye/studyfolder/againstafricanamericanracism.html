What We're About

Racism is a continuing cycle were unaware and uninformed people act based upon the stereotypes and labels given to different races today. In other words, stereotypes displayed in media are affecting the American population and are promoting racist behavior. When looking through the several racist videos next to this section, notice that these videos come in the form of vines and youtube clips, meant to entertain social media users. Then, look at the comments displayed in the section below, and ask, do these comments and clips offend any race or person? The AASF believes that it does. Do you?
If you do believe that these clips and comments offend certain people based upon race, and you wish to stop racism based on social media, then you have come to the right webpage. The AASF's goal is to make more people aware of racism based upon social media and to stop it so that younger generations will not have to experince the racial tension that comes from media.If you wish to know more about us, look at are about us page

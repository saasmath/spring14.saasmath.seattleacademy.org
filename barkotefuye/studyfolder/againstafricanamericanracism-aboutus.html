Our Mission:

There is still racism today due to the several stereotypes spread through the internet and social media. Although people might not be aware of it, racism is still very active in the videos, texts and clips posted on Youtube or other popular sites.People generalize certain races to where they have certain offensive stereotypes.
Our goal is to make our community of Internet users, particularly younger generations, aware of the effects of posting racist clips and comments, and to eventually eradicate racial stereotypes. We believe that stereotypes can lead to false misconceptions that can make a person racist.

Our History:

Just recently, a group of four teenagers, Barkot, Megan, Noah, and Drummound, have decided to create the AASF (Against American Social Racism), and stop stereotyping of races. As teenagers, we wish to convince people our age to support our cause so younger generations will not have to deal with much of the stereotyping that happens today.

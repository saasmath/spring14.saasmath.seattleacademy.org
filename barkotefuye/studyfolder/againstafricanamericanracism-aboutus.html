Our Mission:

We intend to draw attention to these stereotypes so that we can eradicate them. By showing that African American are not fundamentally different, we believe this can lessen and eventually eliminate racism against them. Other organizations do not address stereotypes, and we believe that this is the main reason behind racism so that is why we are attempting to combat it.
